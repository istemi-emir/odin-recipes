Chicken Wrap

Description

This Chicken Wrap recipe is such an easy weeknight dinner.

Done in about 25 minutes and delicious, these will become a regular meal in your house.

  • Cooked Chicken
  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Roma Tomatoes
  1. Cover the chicken breast completely with water and bring to a boil.
  2. Simmer for 4 to 10 minutes. The thickness of the breasts will determine how long you need to cook it.
  3. Cook until it reaches an internal temperature of 165 degrees. I love cooking mine with an instant read thermometer in it. The one linked has a long cord so it can stay in the whole time it is cooking and alert you when the chicken is done.
